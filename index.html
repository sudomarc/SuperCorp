Frequent Questions

Clear answers on our terms and execution framework.

We believe in radical operational transparency. If you have additional technical concerns, contact our principal engineers directly.

What are your baseline project timelines?

Typical project phases range from 4 to 12 weeks depending on structural scale. Simple strategy mapping and prototype scoping take 2 to 4 weeks. Full stack architectures with high-coverage tests generally scale from 6 to 10 weeks.

What technology stack do you deploy?

We prioritize proven, modular, and performant technologies. We build backends using Go, Rust, or Node.js. Frontends are designed with pure Vanilla JavaScript or optimized React ecosystems. Database architectures map directly to PostgreSQL or secure Redis nodes.

What are your billing structures and payment terms?

We operate on clear, flat-fee scopes. Payments are broken down based on milestone execution: 30% upon Scoping phase approval, 40% during core Engineering phases, and 30% upon verified Production handover.

Do you sign standard Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreements (MNDAs)?

Yes. We handle sensitive enterprise data and operational logistics. We sign mutual MNDAs prior to initial discovery scoping calls to secure complete operational protection.
